About

Roberto Garcia Sr. started this company in 2003 with a truck, a set of tools, and a reputation built entirely on word of mouth. He spent twenty years learning the trade across Silver Lake, Echo Park, and Los Feliz — fixing the leaky pipes in old craftsmans, rerouting slab lines, and becoming the guy every neighbor called first.

His son Marco grew up watching his father work. After serving in the United States Marine Corps, he came home and joined the family business — bringing the same discipline and precision from the service into every job they take on.

Today, Garcia & Sons is a tight two-man operation. You'll always get a Garcia on the phone, and a Garcia at your door.
